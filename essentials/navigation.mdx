Timekeepur Labs runs your strategy through an agent swarm and produces artifacts you can execute on immediately.

## 01. Input

Provide a single source of truth to start a diagnostic:

- Resume upload (PDF, DOC, DOCX)
- URL input (LinkedIn, portfolio, or website)
- Natural language prompt inside the War Room

## 02. Engine

Your input is stress-tested by a swarm of generative agents that:

- Observe signals and research from public sources
- Reflect on risks, positioning, and strategy
- Assemble a structured blueprint from the findings

## 03. Export

Once the blueprint is ready, you can ship exports and start execution:

- **Blueprint**: strategy roadmap with recommendations and action steps
- **Presentation (PPTX)**: investor-ready deck export
- **Vision trailer**: cinematic preview of your product vision
- **Cursor deeplink**: open the shipment in Cursor for production work

## Where it shows up

- **Dashboard**: store and revisit blueprints
- **Factory**: create shipments and exports
