## Credit usage

- **Diagnostics / blueprints**: consume credits based on plan limits.
- **Connector syncs**: standard connectors included; premium syncs cost more credits.
- **Factory exports**:
  - PDF + PPTX export: 2 credits
  - Vision trailer: 4 credits

## Seat-based billing

- Plans are billed per seat.
- Credits are allocated per seat each billing period.
- Top-ups never expire (Pro and above).

## Need a custom plan?

Enterprise plans include custom credit allocations, dedicated human + agent teams, and private deployment options. Contact the team to scope your requirements.
