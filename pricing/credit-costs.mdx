## Export credit costs

Every factory export consumes credits. Use this page to estimate usage before you export.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="PDF + PPTX export" icon="file-lines">
    2 credits per export.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Vision trailer" icon="play">
    4 credits per export.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What counts as an export

- Each export action consumes credits once.
- Re-exporting the same blueprint consumes credits again.
- Credits are deducted at the time of export.
